About

“aN-FTea” is a website that serves as a platform for creating and interacting with NFTs. It will also serve as a testing ground in an attempt to create a completely decentralised database. Most importantly, “aN-FTea” will have “Web3.js” at its core, which will act as the building block for DApp.
